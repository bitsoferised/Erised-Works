Share a Book

“Share a Book” is an online platform that is a solution to a problem that many students face. A typical O/L or A/L student comes across difficulties when it comes to finding proper study materials that they require to study for their exams. At times, the book they are looking for is not available in most bookshops or they simply do not have the time to go from bookstore to bookstore looking for a book, thanks to the hectic timetable of an average day in a student’s life. They have the need for not only textbooks but other rare materials such as old past papers and short notes of a peer, but they seldom find these materials. On the other hand, students who are already done with their exams will have a pile of books and other materials that they would love to give out. “Share a Book” bridges this gap.

Current Students

Are you looking for a book that you can’t seem to find anywhere? Let us help you, we can look for it together. “Share a Book” provides you a wide range of resources that you will not find anywhere as a whole bunch together. Browse through bookstore catalogues and search through the bookracks of your peers. Still can’t find it? Don’t just leave, request for it here. Let’s see if someone might have exactly what you need.

Past Students

Are you done with your exams and is now left with a pile of books and notes and what not, that you no longer need? Don’t throw them away, let’s do the environment and your younger brothers and sisters a favour and lend them out. Come join us and upload details about the materials you have here so that another peer of yours who needs it could find it. Giving it for free or for a decent price is your choice. How do you like the idea?

Bookstores

We provide you with a great opportunity to reap more sales by helping you access a large customer base online. The process is simple. Upload your product catalogue (you won’t have any worries because this is the most work you have to do as an initiative and for that too, we will provide you with an easy, convenient and time saving procedure) onto our platform and experience your sales go up when the right customers view your items and purchase them with ease.

Tuition Providers

Are you a small tuition providing entity or individual? If broadcasting awareness regarding the service you provide is an issue, we are your solution. Advertise your service through our platform and build awareness among a wider audience of students. Manifest yourself by providing sample content that proves your calibre and legibility.
